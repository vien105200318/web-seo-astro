# Lessons Learned

## Technical

1. **Start simple**: Vertical slice > full game immediately
2. **Optimize early**: Performance từ đầu, không "sau"
3. **Playtest often**: Own perspective = biased
4. **Version control**: Git saved me countless times

## Creative

5. **Constraints breed creativity**: Limited budget → unique solutions
6. **Cultural authenticity matters**: Don't dilute for mass appeal
7. **Atmosphere > Graphics**: Mood beats polygon count
8. **Sound = 70% of horror**: Invest time here

## Personal

9. **Rest = productivity**: Burnout kills projects
10. **Share journey**: Community support invaluable
11. **Ignore haters**: 5% negative < 95% positive
12. **Health first**: Game không quan trọng bằng sức khỏe

## Business

13. **Solo dev can ship**: Không cần team nếu scope smart
14. **Marketing matters**: Good game + no marketing = nobody knows
15. **Steam works**: Platform democratizes distribution
16. **Passion shows**: Players feel authentic love in product

**Most important: FINISH. Shipped game > perfect idea.**

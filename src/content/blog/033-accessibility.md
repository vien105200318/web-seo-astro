# Accessibility In Horror

## Options Added

- Adjustable subtitle size
- Colorblind modes
- Reduce motion (less camera shake)
- Difficulty settings (ritual complexity)
- Audio cues for horror (cho deaf players)

## Philosophy

Everyone deserves to experience story, dù có disabilities.

## Feedback

Accessibility advocates noticed và praised. Small effort, huge impact.

**Do the right thing.**

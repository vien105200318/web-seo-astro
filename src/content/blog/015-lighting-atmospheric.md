# Lighting Là Mọi Thứ

Thay cubes bằng basic 3D models. Nhìn... meh.

Thêm lighting → TRANSFORMED.

## Setup

- Baked lighting cho static objects
- Real-time shadows cho player
- Volumetric fog
- Warm candlelight (orange 300K)
- Cool moonlight (blue 6500K)

Contrast = tension.

Đã mất 3 ngày chỉ lighting. Worth every second.

**"Lighting makes or breaks your game" - True**
